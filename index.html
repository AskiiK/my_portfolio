About Me

I am a systems-driven transformation leader with over 14 years of deep, cross-functional experience spanning underground mining, national logistics operations, high-scale ed-tech fulfilment, and student experience governance.

My career is defined by a consistent pattern: stepping into complex, multi-team, ambiguous, and often broken environments and turning them into stable, predictable, scalable systems. From the depths of underground mines to the velocity of hyper-scale startups, I build the architecture that allows businesses to grow.

Core Competency: I understand systems end-to-end, simplify complexity, align cross-functional teams without needing authority, and deliver measurable business outcomes.
